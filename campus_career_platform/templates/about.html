About CampusConnect

A campus career platform built around proof of work, visibility, and mentor-driven growth.

Our Mission

CampusConnect is designed to bridge academic effort with career readiness. Instead of leaving student work scattered across chats, labs, and presentation files, the platform turns skills, projects, mentor activity, and progress into one visible campus profile.

Vision

Create a campus ecosystem where every student leaves with a measurable, reviewable professional profile and not just a list of marks or certificates.

Core Idea

Bring together profile building, project proof, skill scoring, reports, and mentor signals in one student-facing workflow that feels useful from day one.

Technology

Flask, Python, JSON-backed persistence with Mongo-ready structure, plus responsive HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a clean demo-to-product transition path.
