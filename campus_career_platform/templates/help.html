Help and Support

Quick guidance for making the most of the platform during demo and usage.

Frequently Asked Questions

The highest-signal actions are now fully implemented.

How do I create a student account?

Open the login page, switch to sign up, and use your institutional email. The platform creates your student profile instantly and redirects you into the main workflow.

Can I update my profile details?

Yes. The dashboard now includes a live edit form for name, headline, department, semester, and skills. Saving updates your report-ready profile data.

How do I add a project?

Use the Add Project panel in the dashboard. New project entries are stored, shown in the portfolio, and reflected in reports and score updates.

What affects the skill score?

The score is influenced by skill coverage, project count, activity signals, mentor presence, and endorsements. It is recalculated when the profile changes.

Demo support note

This version is designed to run cleanly even when MongoDB is unavailable, so you can present the platform confidently without environment blockers.
